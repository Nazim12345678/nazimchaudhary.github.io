End-to-End Supply Chain Data Platform

Client: Starlinks Supply Chain Solutions (KSA & GCC) Role: Lead Data Architect Delivery: 6 months

Overview

Starlinks is a leading supply chain solutions provider delivering high-quality end-to-end logistics management across Saudi Arabia and the GCC region. Built a comprehensive Microsoft Fabric data platform integrating Oracle ERP, Locus, Mintsoft, and Shipy to deliver six core data products addressing critical business challenges across billing, inventory, and operations.

Business Challenge

Starlinks operates across multiple business models (B2B, B2C, 3PL, 4PL) with specialized services including eCommerce fulfillment, automotive logistics, cold-chain management, and SFDA-certified warehousing. Critical challenges included:

  • Fragmented data across Oracle, Locus (3PL last-mile), Mintsoft (eCommerce WMS), and Shipy (eCommerce delivery)
  • Manual billing reconciliation causing revenue leakage
  • No real-time Stock on Hand (SOH) visibility across 15+ warehouses
  • Limited inbound management (IMD) tracking and supplier performance visibility
  • Disconnected last-mile delivery data between 3PL and eCommerce operations
  • Operational inefficiencies with no unified performance metrics

Solution Architecture

Medallion Architecture Implementation

Bronze Layer (Fabric Lakehouse)

  • Real-time integration from Oracle ERP, Locus, Mintsoft, Shipy
  • Fabric Data Factory pipelines for automated extraction
  • Raw data preservation with complete audit trails
  • Historical data archival and compliance tracking

Silver Layer (Fabric Lakehouse)

  • Data quality validation and cleansing
  • Cross-system reconciliation and deduplication
  • Business rules application
  • Conformed dimensions: Customer, Product, Location, Time
  • Slowly Changing Dimensions (SCD Type 2) for historical tracking

Gold Layer (Fabric Data Warehouse)

  • Business-ready data models optimized for analytics
  • Pre-calculated KPIs and metrics
  • Role-based security views
  • Six core data products serving multiple business domains

Data Products Delivered

1. Billing Visibility Data Product

  • Revenue tracking by customer, service line, and region
  • Automated billing reconciliation across all systems
  • Invoice accuracy monitoring and exception handling
  • Revenue leakage detection and prevention

Impact: 85% reduction in billing reconciliation time, eliminated manual processes

2. Stock & Inventory (SOH) Data Product

  • Real-time stock on hand across all warehouse locations
  • Inventory aging analysis and valuation
  • Stock movement tracking and transfer monitoring
  • Reorder point alerts and safety stock management

Impact: 30% reduction in excess inventory, eliminated stockouts through proactive monitoring

3. Inbound Management (IMD) Data Product

  • Inbound shipment tracking from origin to warehouse
  • Receiving operations efficiency monitoring
  • Supplier on-time delivery performance metrics
  • Putaway operations tracking and optimization

Impact: 60% improvement in receiving efficiency, faster putaway operations

4. Last Mile Delivery (LM) Data Product

  • Unified view of 3PL (Locus) and eCommerce (Shipy) deliveries
  • Real-time delivery status and location tracking
  • Driver performance and route optimization analytics
  • Delivery exception management and resolution

Impact: 25% improvement in on-time delivery, 40% reduction in delivery exceptions

5. Outbound Shipment Data Product

  • Order fulfillment tracking from pick to dispatch
  • Picking and packing efficiency metrics
  • Dispatch performance and on-time shipment rates
  • Customer order visibility and tracking

Impact: 50% faster shipment processing, 99.5% order accuracy

6. Operational Efficiency Data Product

  • Warehouse productivity and labor utilization
  • Equipment efficiency and maintenance tracking
  • Process cycle time analysis
  • Cross-dock performance optimization

Impact: 20% operational cost reduction through data-driven optimization
